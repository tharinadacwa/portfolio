01 / About

Engineer first. Curious always.

I'm an Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, working across power electronics, embedded systems, and high-speed PCB design. Most of my projects begin as a question - why this signal is noisier than it should be, why a converter rings where it does - and end on the bench with a board and an oscilloscope.

What I enjoy most is the moment the schematic meets real hardware and the design forces you to be honest about what you got wrong. I care about getting the details right: clean power delivery, controlled impedance, low THD, signal integrity that holds up on a real PDN - not just in simulation.

I'm looking for a hardware development, robotics and automation, embedded systems, digital design internship where I can work on real problems alongside people who hold themselves to that same standard.

Mission

Build hardware that survives contact with reality - measured, debugged, and validated on the bench.

Research Interests

Power conversion & control, high-speed signal integrity, RF & SDR systems, FPGA digital design.

Engineering Focus

Multi-layer PCB layout, closed-loop control, embedded firmware, analog front-end design.
